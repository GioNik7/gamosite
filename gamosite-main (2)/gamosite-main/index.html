    Emporios

    Emporios is a picturesque preserved traditional village on the rim of the caldera situated at an altitude of about 400m, overlooking the Aegean Sea and Volcano. It was mostly abandoned and many houses are in ruins, but recently began to revive and turn into an alternative destination for artistic and sophisticated public (Melanopetra Boutique Apartments) .

    Nikia

    The village of Nikia lies 14 km south of Nisyros, about 400m above sea level in the rim of the crater of the volcano. It is a lovely picturesque area, one of the few crowded settlements in the island. Nikia has an elliptic shape and offers amazing views over the caldera and the sea. The famous village square is paved with fine black pebbles (known in the local as hochlakia) which form a beautiful mosaic.
